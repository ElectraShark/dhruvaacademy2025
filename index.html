About Us

Dhruva Academy is committed to providing high school students with a strong foundation in Science and Math. Our expert teachers, engaging curriculum, and focus on individual attention ensure that every student achieves academic excellence and develops a love for learning.

Established with a vision to nurture young minds, we combine modern teaching techniques with personal mentorship to help students reach their full potential. Join us on a journey of growth, knowledge, and success.

Why Choose Dhruva Academy?

💻 PPT-Based Teaching

Interactive and visual lessons using PowerPoint enhance understanding and save time compared to traditional board teaching.

👥 Low Student Strength

Small batches ensure personalized attention, active participation, and focused guidance for every student.

📝 Online Tests

Short online tests via the Dhruva Academy App with varied formats help students self-evaluate and strengthen learning.

📚 Custom Notes

Faculty-prepared notes aligned with curriculum help in exam preparation and concept clarity.

🧪 Weekly Class Tests

Regular assessments simulate exam conditions and provide continuous feedback to track progress.

🎥 Video Lectures

Recorded sessions via the academy app allow students to review lessons anytime for better retention.
